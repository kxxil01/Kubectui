Kubectui must prioritize performance and responsiveness as a standing product invariant. Future features should be implemented as complete, production-grade canonical paths without sacrificing render-path speed, interaction latency, or overall app responsiveness. Prefer designs that preserve or improve performance while increasing correctness/completeness. For authorization specifically, prefer a tri-state model (Allowed/Denied/Unknown) where mutating or privileged actions fail closed on Unknown, but read-only actions may remain best-effort when necessary to preserve usability. Any feature work that touches hot paths should be validated against the repo performance gates and should avoid regressions by design.
